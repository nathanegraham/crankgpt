The Problem

How much electricity does an AI answer actually take?

AI responses consume real electrical energy, but that cost is completely invisible to the person asking. Cloud AI hides it behind a data center far away.

We wanted to make the energy cost visible — and physical.

Energy AI Measurement Awareness

💡

Energy feels free

When you tap a screen, nothing happens to you. No effort. No feedback.

⚡

But it's not free

Every AI answer draws real watts from a real power grid.

The Solution

Meet the Crankinator

A machine that makes the energy cost of AI real and physical.

  • 1 You type a question into the kiosk
  • 2 A local AI model generates the answer — energy is measured
  • 3 The answer is locked behind an energy meter
  • 4 You crank a generator to produce that exact energy
  • 5 When you've generated enough — the answer unlocks!
⚙️

The Big Idea

"If AI answers cost energy, you should have to make that energy to get the answer."

Research Design

Experiment 1: Energy vs. Answer Length

Question

Does a longer AI answer use more energy?

Procedure

  1. Turn on the Crankinator system
  2. Run 5 trials for each answer length setting
  3. Answer lengths: 25, 50, 100, 200, 400 words
  4. Record: duration + total energy used (mWh)
  5. Subtract idle power to get AI-only energy

Prompt Used

"Why is grass green scientifically in an easy to understand way."

Same prompt every trial — only the word limit changed.

Control Variables

  • ✔ Same AI model every trial
  • ✔ Same prompt every trial
  • ✔ Same hardware / temperature
  • ✔ 5 trials per condition (25 total)

Conclusion

  • 2 Energy per word gets smaller for longer answers (more efficient)
  • 3 Energy scales predictably: ~45 mWh per 100 extra words

Scientific Argument

Claim · Evidence · Reasoning

Claim

AI is not free. It requires real electrical energy to work, and longer answers require significantly more energy.

Evidence

Our measurements showed: a 25-word answer used 20 mWh while a 400-word answer used 188 mWh — a 9× difference. Results were consistent across 5 trials.

Reasoning

The Crankinator makes users generate that exact energy by hand. When you crank for 2 minutes for a long answer, you truly feel what AI costs — proving energy use is real and measurable.

"The Crankinator helps people comprehend the energy cost because they can sense the effort needed to produce it — making invisible electricity visible."
